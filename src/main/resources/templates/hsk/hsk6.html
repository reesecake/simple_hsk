HSK Level 6 Vocabulary List

The HSK level 6 test introduces 2500 new words for a cumulative 5000. 978 new individual characters are added to bring the total to 2663 characters.

Level 6 is designed for learners who can easily understand any information communicated in Chinese and are capable of smoothly expressing themselves in written or oral form.

The HSK level 5 and 6 tests have a maximum of 300 points but, unlike 3 and 4, do not require a minimum score to pass.
