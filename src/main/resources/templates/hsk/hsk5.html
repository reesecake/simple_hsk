HSK Level 5 Vocabulary List

The HSK level 5 test introduces 1300 new words for a cumulative 2500. 621 new individual characters are added to bring the total to 1685 characters.

Level 5 is designed for learners who can read Chinese newspapers and magazines, watch Chinese films and are capable of writing and delivering a lengthy speech in Chinese.

The HSK level 5 and 6 tests have a maximum of 300 points but, unlike 3 and 4, do not require a minimum score to pass.
